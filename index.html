Background


My name is Prescott Rynewicz, and I was born and raised in San Jose California. I am currently a fourth year Aerospace Engineer and Computer Scientist at UCLA. I am passionate about everything Aerospace, as well as software engineering, physical computing, control systems, and general robotics.


Outside of engineering and research, I am an actively engaged musician. I am the president of UCLA's only All Male A Cappella group, Bruin Harmony, and the founder of the UCLA A Cappella Council. I am also a member of the Lambda Chi Alpha Fraternity.


This site serves as a professional profile to display my work experience and project updates.
